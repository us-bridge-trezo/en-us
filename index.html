What is Trézor Bridge™?

Trézor Bridge™ is the official communication interface that links your Trezor® hardware wallet to supported browser-based cryptocurrency applications. Designed for security, stability, and simplicity, it provides a seamless connection between your hardware device and your desktop environment—without compromising privacy or control.

Whether you're trading, storing, or managing assets, Trézor Bridge™ ensures your sensitive operations stay on the device. It replaces outdated browser extensions and gives you direct, encrypted interaction with your crypto portfolio. With automatic updates, native support, and compatibility across major operating systems, Trézor Bridge™ is built for the modern crypto user.

Setup is fast and easy. Download the latest version, install it, and your Trezor® device will instantly become accessible to your favorite apps, such as Trezor Suite®, MetaMask®, and others. Trust in a solution backed by the security philosophy of SatoshiLabs®.
